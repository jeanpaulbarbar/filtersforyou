The Problem

What's Really in Your Sydney Tap Water?

Sydney tap water is treated and safe — but that doesn't mean it's pure. Hundreds of chemicals pass through the treatment plant before reaching your glass. A quality home water filtration system is the most effective solution.

  • ●
    Chlorine & chloramines — affect taste, smell and skin quality every day
  • ●
    Microplastics — found in tap water globally, including Sydney
  • ●
    PFAS "Forever Chemicals" — detected in many Sydney water sources, don't break down
  • ●
    Heavy metals & lead — a real concern in homes with older plumbing
