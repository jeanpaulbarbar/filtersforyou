Common questions

Lifetime warranty, answered

Is the lifetime warranty really free?

Yes. If a covered sealed component fails, we replace the part and do all the labour at no cost, with no call-out fee for a genuine warranty claim. It's backed by your annual service plan — a separate paid service that covers your cartridge replacement and a full inspection each year.

What happens if I miss a service?

You have a 30-day grace period from each service due date. Complete it within that window and your cover carries on unbroken. If a service is left longer than that, the warranty lapses — so we'll always remind you before it's due.

Does the warranty transfer if I sell my home?

No — the lifetime warranty is personal to you at your installation address and doesn't transfer to a new owner or tenant. A new occupant can arrange their own service agreement with us.

Can I get the warranty back after cancelling my plan?

No. Once the plan lapses, the lifetime warranty ends permanently on that system and can't be reinstated — that's what keeps it genuinely sustainable for every client who does stay on plan. You're always welcome back for paid servicing, but the lifetime cover doesn't restart.

Where can I read the full terms?

The complete, binding warranty terms — every condition, exclusion and the claims process — are set out in Section 6 of our Terms & Conditions.
