The Problem We Solve

Why We Do This

Sydney tap water is treated and declared safe — but safe and pure are not the same thing. Jean-Paul saw this firsthand on every job.

Chlorine & Chloramines
Sydney Water adds chlorine and chloramines to every tap. They're effective disinfectants — but they affect taste, smell, and daily water quality in your home.
PFAS Forever Chemicals
PFAS compounds have been detected in Australian water supplies. They don't break down naturally — and a quality reverse osmosis system is the most effective way to remove them.
Microplastics
Microplastics have been found in tap water worldwide, including Australia. They pass through standard treatment. A properly installed filtration system removes them at the source.
Bottled Water Is Not the Answer
The average Sydney family spends $800–$2,000 a year on bottled water. A properly installed filtration system costs a fraction of that and delivers better quality water from every tap.

Jean-Paul founded Filters For You to make the proper solution accessible — a licensed installation, quality WaterMark certified systems, at a fixed upfront price with no surprises.
