Common Problems

Water Filter Problems Jean-Paul Repairs

Leaking Connections or Fittings
Under-sink leaks from push-fit fittings, compression joints or saddle valves. Turn off supply and call immediately to avoid water damage.
Low Water Pressure / Slow Flow
Blocked sediment pre-filters, exhausted carbon cartridges or a depleted RO pressure tank are the most common causes of slow filtered water output.
Bad Taste or Odour Returning
Overdue cartridge replacement or a failed RO membrane allows contaminants through. Jean-Paul tests water quality before and after every repair.
Filter Head Cracks
UV exposure, impact or overtightening can crack plastic filter heads. A cracked head causes leaks and must be replaced to restore performance.
Cartridge Housing Issues
O-ring failures, stripped threads and housing cracks all cause leaks. Jean-Paul carries common housing sizes and O-ring kits for on-the-day repairs.
UV Lamp Replacement
UV lamps lose effectiveness after 12 months regardless of visible light output. Jean-Paul replaces UV lamps and verifies output with a UV meter.
RO Membrane Replacement
RO membranes last 2–3 years under normal Sydney water conditions. Jean-Paul measures TDS before and after replacement to confirm performance is restored.
Pressure Tank Problems
Waterlogged or low-pressure tanks cause very slow RO output. Jean-Paul tests tank pressure, recharges or replaces as needed.

What to Do Before Calling — Immediate Steps

If your water filter is leaking, take these steps immediately before calling Jean-Paul:

  • Turn off the cold water supply valve under your sink — this is a small quarter-turn valve on the cold water line going into your filter system.
  • Place a towel or bucket under the filter unit to catch any residual dripping.
  • Do not attempt to tighten fittings with the supply on — this can worsen the leak or damage connections further.
  • Take a photo of the leak location if safe to do so — it helps Jean-Paul identify the fault before arriving and ensures he brings the right parts.

Once you've secured the leak, call Jean-Paul on 0430 546 749 for same-day or next-business-day attendance.
