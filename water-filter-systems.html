The best system depends on your budget and goals. For taste improvement, the Pure Essential ($550) is a proven entry point. For families wanting maximum contaminant removal — including fluoride, heavy metals and microplastics — the Pure Plus+ ($840) reverse osmosis is our most popular system. For premium alkaline water with hydrogen enrichment, the Pure Premium ($1,180) is the gold standard. Call Jean-Paul on 0430 546 749 for a personalised recommendation.
Yes. Every system in the Filters For You range is WaterMark certified to Australian standards. WaterMark certification ensures the product is safe and fit for its intended use when installed in a plumbing system — a requirement for all plumbing products connected to mains water in NSW.
Yes. In NSW, any connection to the mains cold water supply requires a licensed plumber. Using an unlicensed installer can void your home insurance, the filter warranty, and is non-compliant with NSW plumbing regulations. Jean-Paul holds NSW Plumbing Licence 461511C and personally installs every system.
All prices are fully inclusive — the complete filter system, all fittings, a dedicated filtered water tap, licensed installation by Jean-Paul, and lifetime workmanship warranty. There are no call-out fees, no separate labour charges, and no hidden extras. The price you see is the price you pay.
