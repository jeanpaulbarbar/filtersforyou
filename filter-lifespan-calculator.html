Filter Maintenance Guide

Why Filter Replacement Matters

A water filter that has exceeded its service life is no longer filtering your water effectively. Worse, an overloaded carbon filter can release accumulated contaminants back into your water — the opposite of what it is designed to do. Regular filter changes are not optional maintenance. They are essential to the system working as intended.

The good news is that filter replacement is straightforward and affordable. Jean-Paul from Filters For You offers annual servicing plans that handle cartridge replacement, system inspection and a fresh water quality test.

Signs Your Filter Needs Replacing Now

  • Reduced water flow or pressure from the filter tap
  • Change in water taste or smell
  • It has been more than 12 months since your last service
  • The filter housing appears discoloured or has visible sediment
  • Your smart monitoring system is alerting you
