Why Switch to Filtered Water?

The Real Cost of Bottled Water

Australians spend more than $700 million on bottled water every year. For a family of four buying just two cases of 600ml water per week at around $8 each, that is over $800 a year — and the cost compounds every year you continue.

Beyond the financial cost, consider the environmental impact: plastic water bottles are one of the most significant contributors to landfill and ocean plastic pollution in Australia. A single reverse osmosis system or under-sink filter can eliminate thousands of plastic bottles from your household every year.

How a Home Water Filter Changes the Equation

A professionally installed under-sink water filter from Filters For You costs from $550 — supply and installation included. From the day it is installed, your household has access to clean, filtered water from the kitchen tap at a cost of fractions of a cent per litre.

Reverse osmosis systems (from $840) go even further, removing up to 99% of contaminants including fluoride, chlorine, heavy metals and dissolved solids — producing water quality that rivals or exceeds premium bottled water brands.

What the Calculator Tells You

  • Filter cost — the installed price of the system you selected
  • 5-year savings — the difference between what you would spend on bottled water versus the filter cost
  • Payback period — how many months before the filter has paid for itself through bottled water savings
