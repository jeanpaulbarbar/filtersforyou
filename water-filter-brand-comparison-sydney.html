Why Brand Matters for Sydney Water

Not all water filter brands are equal when it comes to Sydney's specific water supply. The most important factor for Sydney households is chloramine removal. Sydney Water switched from free chlorine to chloramines as the primary disinfectant in 2008. Standard activated carbon media (the type used in most basic filters and imported products) is significantly less effective at removing chloramines than free chlorine.

The FSA and HPF products Jean-Paul installs use certified catalytic carbon or high-contact-time carbon block media specifically rated for chloramine removal. This matters — it's the difference between a filter that genuinely works in Sydney and one that looks the part on paper but underperforms in your actual tap water.

WaterMark Certification — Why It Matters

All plumbing products installed in Australia must be WaterMark certified. This certification verifies that the product is fit for purpose, safe to connect to the water supply, and manufactured to Australian standards. Be cautious of cheap generic filters sold online that may not carry WaterMark certification — these may not be legal for a licensed plumber to install and may not perform as claimed.

Every brand Jean-Paul installs — HPF, FSA, Puretec, Zip and Billi — carries full WaterMark certification and meets all Australian Drinking Water Guidelines.
