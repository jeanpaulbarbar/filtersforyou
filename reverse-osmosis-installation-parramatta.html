Why Parramatta Residents Choose Reverse Osmosis

Parramatta is experiencing one of the most rapid urban transformations in Australian history — thousands of new apartments, a growing CBD, and an increasingly health-conscious, diverse community. For many Parramatta residents, water quality has become a significant concern, particularly in older parts of the district where the mains infrastructure is ageing and more susceptible to sediment, rust and particulate issues.

Reverse osmosis addresses all of this comprehensively. Unlike a simple jug filter or basic inline carbon cartridge, an RO system purifies water at a molecular level — removing dissolved salts, heavy metals, fluoride, nitrates and microplastics in addition to chlorine and sediment. The result is drinking water that is genuinely clean, not just improved.

For Parramatta's growing apartment population, the practical advantages are compelling: an RO system installs entirely under the kitchen sink, connects to the existing cold water supply line, and drains through the existing waste pipe. No mains access is needed. No structural changes are required. In most Parramatta apartment buildings, no strata approval is necessary.

Parramatta's Water Quality Challenge

Parramatta is served by Sydney Water through the western distribution network — older infrastructure serving a dense urban population. While Sydney Water consistently meets Australian Drinking Water Guidelines, Parramatta tap water contains:

  • Chloramines — secondary disinfectants with a persistent, distinctive taste and odour that many western Sydney residents notice
  • Fluoride — added for public dental health at approximately 0.6–1.0 mg/L; some households prefer to filter it out
  • Sediment and rust — more prevalent in Parramatta's older building stock and mains infrastructure
  • Heavy metals — lead and copper can leach from older plumbing and building materials in Parramatta's established residential areas
  • Microplastics — detected in Australian urban water supplies with no current maximum guideline

A 5-stage reverse osmosis system removes all of these before the water reaches your glass. The taste difference after installation is immediately noticeable.
