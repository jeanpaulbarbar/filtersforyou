Water Quality in Ashfield: What Inner West Residents Are Drinking

Ashfield's water is supplied by Sydney Water through the Inner West distribution network — the same supply that serves neighbouring Burwood, Croydon Park, Leichhardt and Newtown. It meets Australian Drinking Water Guidelines, but many Ashfield residents notice the chlorine and chloramine taste, particularly in hot drinks and in water left to stand.

Sydney Water's annual reports confirm that treatment chemicals, disinfection byproducts, and trace contaminants are present in the supply. For Ashfield families — particularly those with young children, pregnant women, or health-conscious individuals — a water filtration system removes these compounds before they reach the kitchen tap.

The most noticeable improvement is in taste and smell. After installation, most customers report that their water tastes noticeably cleaner and that the chlorine smell from the hot tap disappears. But the less visible benefits are equally important: reduced chloramines, lower disinfection byproduct exposure, and elimination of microplastics that pass through Sydney Water's treatment process.

Reverse Osmosis for Ashfield Families: Is It Worth It?

Reverse osmosis is consistently the upgrade choice for Ashfield households who've thought seriously about their water quality. Where a standard carbon filter makes water taste better by removing chlorine, a reverse osmosis system genuinely purifies it — removing dissolved contaminants that a carbon filter can't touch.

The Pure Plus+ 5-Stage RO at $840 installed is Jean-Paul's most recommended system for Ashfield families. It removes chloramines (not just chlorine), fluoride, heavy metals including lead and mercury, nitrates, and microplastics. The water it produces is comparable to quality bottled water — except it comes from your own tap, at a fraction of the ongoing cost.

The only trade-off is flow rate: RO systems produce water slowly and store it in a small pressure tank under the sink. For drinking and cooking, this is entirely sufficient for family use. Jean-Paul walks you through the full system at installation, including when and how to replace filter cartridges.
