About The System

Know Your Water Quality. Automatically.

Reverse osmosis is the most comprehensive residential water filtration method available, but it works invisibly. You turn the tap, draw filtered water, and trust that the system is performing. With the Pure Luxe, you don't have to trust blindly. The built-in smart monitor continuously tests your water quality (TDS) and shows you exactly how the system is performing in real time.

Sydney mains water typically reads 80–180 ppm TDS. A well-maintained RO system should deliver under 20 ppm. The Pure Luxe's smart monitor watches your water quality continuously and gives you a set-and-forget reminder when the filters are due. It also has built-in leak detection that automatically shuts off the water supply if it ever senses an internal leak. When the TDS reading starts creeping up, you know the membrane is due for service, before your water quality actually degrades.

On top of the monitoring capability, the Pure Luxe uses the same quick-change twist-fit cartridge system as the Pure Advanced, no tools required for annual servicing. All five cartridges are accessible under the sink and replaced with a simple quarter-turn. The RO membrane is the only stage that typically requires a service visit (every 3–5 years).

Jean-Paul installs every Pure Luxe personally. Fixed price of $1,520 includes the system, 12L tank, smart monitor, booster pump, all fittings, and licensed installation.
